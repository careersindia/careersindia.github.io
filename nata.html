NATA

Full Form
National Aptitude Test in Architecture
Eligibility
Passed 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry; or 10+3 Diploma with Mathematics.
Minimum 50% aggregate marks.
No age limit.
Scope
Qualifier for B.Arch programs in India (except IITs, NITs, and SPAs).
How to Apply
Online via nata.in
Exam Structure
- Part A: Drawing Test (Pen and paper).
- Part B: PCM and General Aptitude (Computer-based).
Score Pattern & Analysis
- Total Marks: 200
- Part A: 125 marks
- Part B: 75 marks
- Qualifying marks determined by CoA.
Fees
- ₹2,000 per attempt (General)
- ₹1,500 per attempt (SC/ST/PwD)
Exam Timing
Multiple sessions between March and June 2025
Frequency
Multiple times a year
Result Time
Within 3 weeks of exam
