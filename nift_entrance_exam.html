NIFT Entrance Exam

Full Form
National Institute of Fashion Technology Entrance Exam
Eligibility
- UG: Passed/appearing 10+2; age ≤24 years as of Aug 1, 2024 (5-year relaxation for SC/ST/PwD).
- PG: Bachelor's degree; no age limit.
Scope
Admission to UG & PG programs at NIFT campuses.
How to Apply
Online via nift.ac.in
Exam Structure
- UG: GAT (General Ability Test) + CAT (Creative Ability Test) + Situation Test.
- PG: GAT + CAT + GD/PI.
Score Pattern & Analysis
- GAT: 30%
- CAT: 50%
- Situation Test/GD-PI: 20%
Fees
- ₹3,000 (General)
- ₹1,500 (SC/ST/PwD)
Exam Timing
Feb 9, 2025
Frequency
Once a year
Result Time
March 2025
