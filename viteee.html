VITEEE

Name of Entrance Test
VITEEE
Full Form
Vellore Institute of Technology Engineering Entrance Examination
Eligibility
10+2 with PCM/PCB; 60% aggregate (50% for SC/ST, some states)
Scope
Admission to VIT campuses (Vellore, Chennai, Bhopal, AP)
How to Apply
Online via https://viteee.vit.ac.in
Exam Structure
Online; 125 questions – PCM/B + Aptitude + English
Score Pattern/Analysis
No negative marking; each correct = 1 mark; percentile-based rank
Fees
₹1350 (approx)
Time When Conducted
April
Number of Times/Year
1
Time for Results
~2 weeks after exam
