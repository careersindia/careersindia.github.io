DBT JRF

Full Form
Department of Biotechnology Junior Research Fellowship
Eligibility
M.Sc./M.Tech in Life Sciences/Biotech with 60% (Gen), 55% (SC/ST/OBC)
Scope
JRF for Ph.D. in DBT-supported institutions
How to Apply
Apply at rcb.res.in
Exam Structure
2 Sections: Part A (General Science), Part B (Biotech); 3 hours
Score Pattern & Analysis
+3 for correct, -1 for incorrect. Selection into Category I (JRF) or II.
Fees
₹1200 (Gen/OBC), ₹600 (SC/ST), ₹0 (PwD)
Time Conducted
Usually in March–April
Frequency
Once a year
Result Time
Results in 5–6 weeks
