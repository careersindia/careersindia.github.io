Ph.D. Programs and MD Degrees Overview

1. Ph.D. in Literature

Semester-wise Syllabus

  • Sem 1: Research Methodology in Literary Studies, Literary Theories, Academic Writing
  • Sem 2: Comparative Literature, Postcolonial Studies, Textual Criticism
  • Sem 3: Thesis Proposal, Archival Research, Seminar Presentation
  • Sem 4: Drafting & Peer Review, Ethics, Journal Submission
  • Sem 5: Chapter Completion, Conference Paper, Teaching Methods
  • Sem 6: Final Thesis, Viva, Publishing

Main Topics

  • Literary Theories (Structuralism, Postmodernism)
  • Comparative Literature
  • Cultural and Postcolonial Critique
  • Critical Reading & Analysis
  • Textual Studies

Scope

  • Academia, Editorial roles, Publishing, Media, Scriptwriting

Salary

  • Entry: ₹35K–₹55K/month
  • Mid: ₹70K–₹1.2L/month
  • Senior: ₹1.5L–₹2.5L+/month
