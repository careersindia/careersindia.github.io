CMAT – Common Management Admission Test

Eligibility
Bachelor’s degree in any discipline. Final-year students can apply.
Scope
Admission to AICTE-approved institutions across India.
How to Apply
Online registration via the NTA CMAT website.
Exam Structure
Five sections: Quantitative Techniques & Data Interpretation, Logical Reasoning, Language Comprehension, General Awareness, Innovation & Entrepreneurship. Duration: 180 minutes. Computer-based test.
Score Pattern
Each section carries 100 marks; total of 500 marks.
Fees
Approximately ₹2,000 for General category; ₹1,000 for reserved categories.
Exam Timing
Conducted once a year, typically in January.
Results
Announced within a few weeks after the exam.
