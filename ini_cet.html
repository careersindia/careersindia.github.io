INI-CET

Name of Test
INI-CET
Full Form
Institute of National Importance Combined Entrance Test
Eligibility
MBBS/BDS with 1-year internship completed; 55% (Gen), 50% (SC/ST)
Scope
Admission to MD/MS/MCh (6yrs)/DM (6yrs)/MDS in AIIMS, JIPMER, PGIMER, NIMHANS
How to Apply
Online via https://www.aiimsexams.ac.in
Exam Structure
Online CBT; 200 questions; single correct/multiple correct types
Score Pattern/Analysis
+1 correct, -1/3 wrong; percentile score; merit-based
Fees
₹4000 (General/OBC); ₹3200 (SC/ST/EWS); PwD – Nil
Time Conducted
Twice – January & July sessions
Times/Year
2
Result Time
~2 weeks after exam
