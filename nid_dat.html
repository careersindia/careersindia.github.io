NID DAT

Full Form
National Institute of Design – Design Aptitude Test
Eligibility
- B.Des: Passed/appearing 10+2; age ≤20 years (General/EWS/OBC-NCL), ≤23 years (SC/ST/PwD) as of July 1, 2024.
- M.Des: Bachelor's degree/diploma (min. 3 years) or equivalent; final year students eligible.
Scope
Admission to B.Des & M.Des programs at NID campuses.
How to Apply
Online via admissions.nid.edu
Exam Structure
- Prelims: MCQs + subjective questions.
- Mains: Studio Test + Interview.
Score Pattern & Analysis
- Prelims: 100 marks.
- Mains: 100 marks.
- Final merit based on combined scores.
Fees
- ₹3,000 (General)
- ₹1,500 (SC/ST/PwD)
Exam Timing
- Prelims: Jan 5, 2025
- Mains: April 2025
Frequency
Once a year
Result Time
- Prelims: Feb 2025
- Final: May 2025
