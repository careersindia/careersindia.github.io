KVPY (Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana)

Full Form
Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana
Eligibility
Students enrolled in XI, XII (Science subjects), and 1st year of B.Sc./B.S./B.Stat./B.Math./Int. M.Sc./M.S.
Scope
Fellowship program to encourage students to take up research careers in Basic Sciences. Admission to IISc, IISERs etc. considered KVPY fellows for some channels.
How to Apply
Online via the official KVPY website (kvpy.iisc.ernet.in - now likely directs to INSPIRE info).
Exam Structure
Aptitude test (Computer-based). Questions from Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology.
Score Pattern/Analysis
Marks based on performance in aptitude test, followed by an interview for shortlisted candidates (interview stage was sometimes waived).
Fees
₹1250 (Gen/OBC); ₹625 (SC/ST/PwD).
Time When Conducted
Usually in November.
Number of Times/Year
1.
Time for Results
Aptitude test results in December/January, final results after interviews.
Note: As of 2022, the KVPY aptitude test has been discontinued and subsumed by INSPIRE-SHE.
