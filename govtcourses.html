I. Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) Exams

Civil Services Examination

  • Indian Administrative Service (IAS)
  • Indian Police Service (IPS)
  • Indian Foreign Service (IFS)
  • Indian Revenue Service (IRS), and various other Group 'A' and 'B' Central Services.

Indian Forest Service (IFoS) Examination

Recruits for: Officers in the Indian Forest Service.

Combined Defence Services (CDS) Examination

Recruits for:

  • Officers in the Indian Army, Navy, and Air Force (Permanent Commission & Short Service Commission).

Central Armed Police Forces (CAPF) (Assistant Commandants) Examination

Recruits for:

  • Assistant Commandants in BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP, SSB.

Engineering Services Examination (ESE) / IES

Recruits for:

  • Group 'A' and 'B' posts in various engineering disciplines in Central Government departments (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics & Telecommunication).

Combined Geo-Scientist Examination

Recruits for:

  • Geologist, Geophysicist, Chemist, and Hydrogeologist posts in Geological Survey of India and Central Ground Water Board.

Statistical Service Examination (ISS) / Indian Economic Service (IES)

Recruits for:

  • Junior Scale posts in Indian Statistical Service and Indian Economic Service.

Combined Medical Services (CMS) Examination

Recruits for:

  • Medical Officers in various central government organizations.

Eligibility:

MBBS Degree.

II. Staff Selection Commission (SSC) Exams

SSC conducts exams for various Group 'B' and 'C' posts in ministries, departments, and subordinate offices of the Government of India.

SSC Combined Graduate Level (CGL) Examination

Recruits for:

  • Assistant Audit Officer, Assistant Accounts Officer, Income Tax Inspector, Central Excise Inspector, Assistant Section Officer, Sub-Inspector (CBI), Auditor, Accountant, Statistical Investigator, etc.

SSC Combined Higher Secondary Level (CHSL) Examination

Recruits for:

  • Lower Division Clerk (LDC), Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA), Postal Assistant (PA), Sorting Assistant (SA), Data Entry Operator (DEO).

SSC Multi-Tasking (Non-Technical) Staff (MTS) Examination

Recruits for:

  • Peon, Daftary, Jamadar, Junior Gestetner Operator, Chowkidar, Safaiwala, Mali, etc.

SSC Stenographer (Grade 'C' & 'D') Examination

Recruits for:

  • Stenographers in various ministries/departments.

SSC GD Constable Examination

Recruits for:

  • Constables (General Duty) in BSF, CISF, ITBP, CRPF, NCB, SSF, and Rifleman (GD) in Assam Rifles.

SSC CPO (Central Police Organization) Examination

Recruits for:

  • Sub-Inspector in Delhi Police, CAPFs, and Assistant Sub-Inspector in CISF.

SSC Junior Engineer (JE) Examination

Recruits for:

  • Junior Engineers (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Quantity Surveying & Contracts) in various government departments.

III. Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) Exams

RRBs conduct recruitment for various technical and non-technical posts in the Indian Railways.

RRB Non-Technical Popular Categories (NTPC) Examination

Recruits for:

  • Junior Clerk cum Typist, Accounts Clerk cum Typist, Junior Time Keeper, Trains Clerk, Commercial cum Ticket Clerk, Goods Guard, Senior Commercial cum Ticket Clerk, Senior Clerk cum Typist, Junior Account Assistant cum Typist, Senior Time Keeper, Commercial Apprentice, Station Master.

Eligibility:

  • 12th Pass (for undergraduate posts), Bachelor's Degree (for graduate posts).

RRB Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP) & Technicians Examination

Recruits for:

  • Assistant Loco Pilots and various Technician posts.

Eligibility:

10th Pass + ITI / Diploma / Degree in relevant engineering trades.

RRB Junior Engineer (JE) Examination

Recruits for:

  • Junior Engineer, Junior Engineer (IT), Depot Material Superintendent (DMS), Chemical & Metallurgical Assistant (CMA).

Eligibility:

Diploma / Degree in Engineering in the relevant discipline.

RRB Group D Examination

Recruits for:

  • Track Maintainer Grade-IV, Helper/Assistant, Assistant Pointsman, Hospital Assistant, etc.

Eligibility:

10th Pass / ITI.

RRB Paramedical Categories Examination

Recruits for:

  • Dietitian, Staff Nurse, Health & Malaria Inspector, Lab Superintendent, Optometrist, etc.

Railway Protection Force (RPF) Constable/Sub-Inspector Examination

Recruits for:

  • Constables and Sub-Inspectors in RPF/RPSF.

IV. Banking Sector Exams

Various public sector banks (PSBs), including SBI and cooperative banks, conduct recruitment drives.

IBPS (Institute of Banking Personnel Selection) Exams

  • IBPS PO (Probationary Officer): Recruits for POs in various PSBs.
  • IBPS Clerk: Recruits for Clerical Cadre in various PSBs.
  • IBPS SO (Specialist Officer): Recruits for IT Officer, Agricultural Field Officer, Law Officer, HR/Personnel Officer, Marketing Officer, Rajbhasha Adhikari.
  • IBPS RRB (Regional Rural Banks) Officer (Scale I, II, III) & Office Assistant (Multipurpose): Recruits for various posts in RRBs.

Eligibility:

Bachelor's Degree for most posts. Specific professional degrees for SO posts.

SBI (State Bank of India) Exams

  • SBI PO (Probationary Officer)
  • SBI Clerk (Junior Associate)
  • SBI SO (Specialist Officer)

Eligibility:

Bachelor's Degree for most posts. Specific professional degrees for SO posts.

RBI (Reserve Bank of India) Exams

  • RBI Grade B Officer: Recruits for officers in various departments of RBI.
  • RBI Assistant: Recruits for Assistant posts.

NABARD (National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development) Exams

  • NABARD Grade A (Assistant Manager): Recruits for various disciplines.
  • NABARD Grade B (Manager): Recruits for various disciplines.

Eligibility:

Bachelor's Degree/Master's Degree in relevant fields.

V. State Public Service Commission (PSC) Exams

Each state in India has its own Public Service Commission (e.g., UPPSC, MPSC, TNPSC, RPSC, KPSC, BPSC, WBPSC) that conducts exams for various state government services.

State Civil Services Exams (e.g., PCS, RAS, KAS, WBCS)

Recruits for:

  • SDM, DSP, Tehsildar, Block Development Officer, various Class I & II state-level administrative posts.

State Judicial Services Exams (PCS-J / HJS)

Recruits for:

  • Civil Judge (Junior Division) / Judicial Magistrate (PCS-J) and Additional District & Sessions Judge (HJS).

Eligibility:

  • LLB Degree (for PCS-J), LLB with 7+ years of practice (for HJS).

State Forest Service Exams

Recruits for:

  • Forest Rangers, Assistant Conservators of Forests.

Eligibility:

Bachelor's Degree in Science/Engineering/Agriculture/Forestry.

Other State-Specific Exams

For various departments like Education, Health, Agriculture, Police, Revenue, etc., at different levels (clerical, subordinate services, officer). Eligibility varies widely by post.

VI. Defence & Paramilitary Forces (Other than UPSC CDS/CAPF & SSC GD)

Indian Army Agniveer (Various Trades)

Recruits for:

  • Soldiers in various categories (General Duty, Technical, Clerk/Store Keeper, Tradesman).

Eligibility:

10th/12th Pass depending on trade.

Indian Navy Agniveer (SSR/MR)

Recruits for:

  • Sailors for Senior Secondary Recruit (SSR) and Matric Recruit (MR).

Indian Air Force Agniveer (Agniveer Vayu - Group X & Y)

Recruits for:

  • Airmen in Technical and Non-Technical trades.

Territorial Army (TA) Officer/JCO/Other Ranks

Recruits for:

  • Part-time military service while maintaining civilian careers.

Various Paramilitary Forces Recruitment (BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP, SSB, Assam Rifles)

Recruits for:

  • Constables, Head Constables, Sub-Inspectors, Assistant Sub-Inspectors in various trades (GD, Tradesman, Driver, Ministerial, Technical, etc.).

Intelligence Bureau (IB) Exams

  • IB ACIO (Assistant Central Intelligence Officer): Officer-level posts.
  • IB Security Assistant/Executive: Non-gazetted posts.

VII. Other Government & Public Sector Undertaking (PSU) Exams

Insurance Sector Exams

  • LIC AAO (Assistant Administrative Officer), ADO (Apprentice Development Officer)
  • NIACL (New India Assurance Co. Ltd) AO (Administrative Officer)
  • UIIC (United India Insurance Co. Ltd) AO
  • OICL (Oriental Insurance Co. Ltd) AO

Food Corporation of India (FCI) Exams

Recruits for various posts in FCI.

Department of Posts (India Post)

  • Gramin Dak Sevak (GDS): Merit-based selection from 10th marks, no exam.
  • Postman/Mail Guard, Multi-Tasking Staff (MTS)
  • Postal Assistant/Sorting Assistant

Comptroller and Auditor General of India (CAG)

Recruits for: Auditor, Accountant, Junior Accountant (often through SSC CGL/CHSL).

Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB)

Recruits for various posts within the NCB.

Directorate of Enforcement (ED)

Recruits for various posts within the ED.
