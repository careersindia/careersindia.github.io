CUET UG

Full Form
Common University Entrance Test Undergraduate
Eligibility
12th pass or appearing. No age limit.
Scope
Admission to UG courses in Central, State, Private, and Deemed universities
How to Apply
Register on cuet.samarth.ac.in
Exam Structure
Multiple choice, Section I (Languages), II (Domain), III (General Test); duration varies by subject combinations
Score Pattern & Analysis
+5 for correct, -1 for incorrect. Normalized scores across shifts.
Fees
₹750 (Gen), ₹700 (OBC/EWS), ₹650 (SC/ST/PwD) for up to 3 subjects
Time Conducted
May–June
Frequency
Once a year
Result Time
Results in 4–6 weeks
