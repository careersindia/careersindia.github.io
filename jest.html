JEST

Full Form
Joint Entrance Screening Test
Eligibility
B.E./B.Tech/M.Sc. in Physics/Maths/Computational Science (depends on program)
Scope
Admission to Ph.D. & integrated Ph.D. in physics & theoretical computer science in IISER, NISER, etc.
How to Apply
Apply at jest.org.in
Exam Structure
Single paper: 3 sections (Part A: Math, B: Physics, C: Advanced); 3 hours
Score Pattern & Analysis
+1 for correct (in Parts A & B), +3 (Part C), negative marking varies
Fees
₹800 (Gen), ₹400 (SC/ST/Female)
Time Conducted
Typically in February
Frequency
Once a year
Result Time
Results in 4–5 weeks
