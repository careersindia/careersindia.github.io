XAT – Xavier Aptitude Test

Eligibility
Bachelor’s degree in any discipline. Final-year students can apply.
Scope
Admission to XLRI and over 250 B-schools in India.
How to Apply
Online registration via the official XAT website from July to November.
Exam Structure
Part 1: Verbal & Logical Ability, Decision Making, Quantitative Ability & Data Interpretation. Part 2: General Knowledge. Duration: 210 minutes. Computer-based test.
Score Pattern
Scores for Part 1 used for percentile calculation; Part 2 scores considered by some institutes.
Fees
Approximately ₹2,100.
Exam Timing
Conducted once a year, typically in January.
Results
Announced in January.
