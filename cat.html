CAT – Common Admission Test

Eligibility
Bachelor’s degree with at least 50% marks (45% for SC/ST/PwD). Final-year students can apply.
Scope
Admission to IIMs and over 1,200 B-schools in India.
How to Apply
Online registration via the official CAT website during August–September.
Exam Structure
Three sections: Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension, Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning, Quantitative Ability. Duration: 120 minutes. Computer-based test.
Score Pattern
Scaled scores and percentiles for each section and overall.
Fees
Approximately ₹2,400 for General category; ₹1,200 for SC/ST/PwD.
Exam Timing
Conducted once a year, typically in November.
Results
Announced in January.
