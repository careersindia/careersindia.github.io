IISER Aptitude Test (IAT)

Full Form
Indian Institutes of Science Education and Research Aptitude Test
Eligibility
Candidates who have passed 10+2 level with science stream in the current or previous year. Minimum marks criteria apply (varies, usually around 60% for GEN/OBC, 55% for SC/ST/PwD).
Scope
Admission to 5-year BS-MS dual degree programs at various IISERs (Berhampur, Bhopal, Kolkata, Mohali, Pune, Thiruvananthapuram, Tirupati).
How to Apply
Online via the official IISER admissions website (e.g., iiseradmission.in).
Exam Structure
Computer-based test. 60 questions (15 each from Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology). Candidates can attempt any three or all four sections. Best three scores considered if all four are attempted for some channels.
Score Pattern/Analysis
+4 for correct, -1 for incorrect. Total marks: 240.
Fees
₹2000 (Gen/OBC-NCL/EWS); ₹1000 (SC/ST/PwD/KM).
Time When Conducted
Usually in June.
Number of Times/Year
1
Time for Results
~2-3 weeks after the exam.
