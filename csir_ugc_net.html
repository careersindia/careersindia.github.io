CSIR UGC NET

Full Form
Council of Scientific & Industrial Research – University Grants Commission National Eligibility Test
Eligibility
M.Sc./BE/BTech/Integrated BS-MS or equivalent with min 55% (Gen), 50% (SC/ST/OBC).
Scope
Eligibility for JRF or Lectureship in science subjects (e.g., Life Sciences, Physical Sciences, Chemical Sciences)
How to Apply
Apply at csirnet.nta.nic.in
Exam Structure
3 parts: A (General), B & C (Subject-specific); 3 hours
Score Pattern & Analysis
+2 for correct, -0.5 for incorrect. Cut-offs vary per subject and category.
Fees
₹1100 (Gen), ₹550 (OBC), ₹275 (SC/ST/PwD)
Time Conducted
June & December
Frequency
Twice a year
Result Time
Results in 4–6 weeks
