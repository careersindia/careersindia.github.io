NEST (National Entrance Screening Test)

Full Form
National Entrance Screening Test
Eligibility
10+2 (Science stream) passed or appearing, with at least 60% (General/OBC) or 55% (SC/ST/PwD) aggregate marks. Age limits apply.
Scope
Admission to 5-year integrated M.Sc. programs at National Institute of Science Education and Research (NISER), Bhubaneswar, and University of Mumbai - Department of Atomic Energy Centre for Excellence in Basic Sciences (UM-DAE CEBS), Mumbai.
How to Apply
Online via the official NEST website (e.g., nestexam.in).
Exam Structure
Computer-based test. 4 sections (Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, Physics) + 1 General section (compulsory). Each subject section has 50 marks. General section has 30 marks. Total 230 marks.
Score Pattern/Analysis
Different marking for different types of questions (MCQ, MSQ). Merit list based on percentile scores, considering general section and best 3 subject scores.
Fees
₹1400 (Male - Gen/OBC); ₹700 (SC/ST/PwD/Female - all categories).
Time When Conducted
Usually in June.
Number of Times/Year
1.
Time for Results
~2-3 weeks after the exam.
