LSAT India – Law School Admission Test India

Eligibility
Candidates must have completed 10+2 (or equivalent) examinations from a recognized board or institution.
Scope
Accepted by over 50 law colleges in India for admission to 5-year integrated LLB, 3-year LLB, and LLM programs.
How to Apply
Online registration via the official LSAT India website.
Exam Structure
Sections include Analytical Reasoning, Logical Reasoning (1 & 2), and Reading Comprehension. Duration: Approximately 2 hours and 20 minutes. Computer-based test.
Score Pattern
Scores range from 420 to 480, along with a percentile rank.
Fees
Approximately ₹3,999.
Exam Timing
Conducted twice a year, typically in January and May.
Results
Announced within a few weeks after the exam.
