BITSAT

Name of Entrance Test
BITSAT
Full Form
Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test
Eligibility
10+2 with PCM and 75% aggregate; proficiency in English
Scope
Admission to BITS Pilani, Goa, Hyderabad campuses
How to Apply
Online via https://www.bitsadmission.com
Exam Structure
Online test; 150 questions – PCM, English Proficiency, Logical Reasoning
Score Pattern/Analysis
+3 correct, -1 wrong; 450 total; additional 12 questions if all attempted
Fees
₹3400 (Male), ₹2900 (Female); ₹7000 for retest
Time When Conducted
May–July (2 sessions)
Number of Times/Year
2
Time for Results
Immediately after test (scorecard), merit list in ~2–3 weeks
