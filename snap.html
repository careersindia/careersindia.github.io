SNAP – Symbiosis National Aptitude Test

Eligibility
Bachelor’s degree with at least 50% marks (45% for SC/ST). Final-year students can apply.
Scope
Admission to MBA programs offered by Symbiosis International University and its constituent institutes.
How to Apply
Online registration via the official SNAP website.
Exam Structure
Three sections: General English, Analytical & Logical Reasoning, Quantitative, Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency. Duration: 60 minutes. Computer-based test.
Score Pattern
Each correct answer awards 1 mark; 0.25 marks deducted for each wrong answer.
Fees
Approximately ₹2,250 per attempt.
Exam Timing
Conducted once a year, typically in December, with up to three attempts allowed.
Results
Announced in January.
