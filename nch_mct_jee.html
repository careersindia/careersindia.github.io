NCHMCT JEE

Full Form
National Council for Hotel Management and Catering Technology Joint Entrance Examination
Eligibility
12th pass with English as a subject. Max age: 25 years (Gen), 28 (SC/ST).
Scope
Admission to B.Sc. in Hospitality & Hotel Administration in IHMs.
How to Apply
Apply on nta.ac.in or nchmjee.nta.nic.in
Exam Structure
200 questions (Numerical Ability, Reasoning, GK, English, Aptitude for Service Sector) – 3 hours
Score Pattern & Analysis
+4 for correct, -1 for incorrect. Merit list based on total marks.
Fees
Gen/OBC: ₹1000, EWS: ₹700, SC/ST/PwD: ₹450
Time Conducted
Usually in May
Frequency
Once a year
Result Time
Results usually in 3–4 weeks
