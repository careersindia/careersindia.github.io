CEED

Full Form
Common Entrance Examination for Design
Eligibility
Bachelor's degree/diploma (min. 3 years) or equivalent; final year students eligible.
No age limit.
Scope
Admission to M.Des programs at IITs, IISc, and other institutes.
How to Apply
Online via ceed.iitb.ac.in
Exam Structure
Single paper with two parts:
- Part A: NAT, MSQ, MCQ (Computer-based).
- Part B: Drawing (Pen and paper).
Score Pattern & Analysis
Total Marks: 200
- Part A: 100 marks
- Part B: 100 marks
- Part A used for shortlisting; Part B evaluated for final score.
Fees
- ₹3,600 (General)
- ₹1,800 (SC/ST/PwD)
Exam Timing
Jan 19, 2025
Frequency
Once a year
Result Time
March 2025
