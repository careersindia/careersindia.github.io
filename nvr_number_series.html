Number Figure Series

What is a Number Figure Series?

Number Figure Series questions combine logical number sequences with visual figures. The challenge is to identify the relationship between the numbers and the shapes they are in, or the relationship between numbers in different positions within a shape.

How to Solve These Puzzles

The logic can be simple or complex. Systematically check for these common patterns:

  • Arithmetic Operations: Look for patterns like addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, squares, or cubes between the numbers in the sequence.
  • Positional Logic: The numbers in different parts of a figure might be related. For example, the sum/product of numbers on the outside equals the number on the inside.
  • Figure Property Logic: The number might be related to a property of the figure itself, such as the number of sides, vertices, or intersecting lines.
  • Interleaved Series: There might be two or more independent series running in parallel within the figures.
  • Sum of Digits: The pattern might be based on the sum of the digits of the numbers.
