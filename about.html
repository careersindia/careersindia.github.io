About Our App

Welcome to Careers India, your definitive career and education navigator, meticulously designed as a Progressive Web App (PWA) to guide you through every critical decision-making milestone. Our mission is to eliminate confusion and empower students with a 360-degree view of their academic and professional journey.

For students at a crossroads, we offer exhaustive roadmaps for paths after the 10th, 12th, graduation, and post-graduation. Uniquely, we also provide well-researched "Plan B" alternative career options at each stage, ensuring you always have a viable and promising direction. Dive deep into specialized sections covering prestigious government jobs, a vast database of entrance exams, and a complete guide to studying abroad. Our international section details every step of the study abroad process, from university selection to visa applications, and includes practical guides on living and learning abroad. To connect you with experts, we also feature a curated directory of top study abroad consultants.

Our platform is distinguished by a suite of powerful, data-driven tools designed to provide clarity and confidence. Discover your ideal career matches with Udaan, our insightful psychometric test. Make financially sound choices using EduCalc, which calculates the cost-income ratio and payback period of various courses, and evaluate options side-by-side with our intuitive Course Comparison tool. Gauge real-world demand with our unique Perception Rating for courses, and identify and bridge crucial skill gaps with PrepCheck.

Stay organized with our private College Application Tracker and explore thousands of opportunities in our extensive scholarship directories for both India and abroad. With features like Campus Count providing institutional data, an AI Counselor for instant advice, and rich resources on emerging careers, Careers India is more than just an app. It's an indispensable ecosystem of information, tools, and guidance, built to ensure every student can architect a clear and successful path to their future.
