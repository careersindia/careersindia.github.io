SLAT – Symbiosis Law Admission Test

Eligibility
Candidates must have completed 10+2 (or equivalent) examinations from a recognized board or institution with a minimum of 45% marks (40% for SC/ST).
Scope
Admission to Symbiosis Law Schools in Pune, Noida, Hyderabad, and Nagpur.
How to Apply
Online registration via the official Symbiosis Entrance Test (SET) website.
Exam Structure
Five sections: Logical Reasoning, Legal Reasoning, Analytical Reasoning, Reading Comprehension, General Knowledge. Total of 60 multiple-choice questions. Duration: 1 hour. Computer-based test.
Score Pattern
Each correct answer awards 1 mark; no negative marking.
Fees
Approximately ₹2,250.
Exam Timing
Conducted once a year, typically in May.
Results
Announced approximately ten days after the exam.
