ICMR JRF

Full Form
Indian Council of Medical Research Junior Research Fellowship
Eligibility
M.Sc./MA in basic or applied science with 55% marks (Gen), 50% (SC/ST/OBC)
Scope
JRF in biomedical sciences for Ph.D. in ICMR institutes and universities
How to Apply
Apply at pgimer.edu.in or ICMR's site
Exam Structure
2 Sections: Section A (Aptitude), Section B/C (Life Sciences/Social Sciences); 2 hours
Score Pattern & Analysis
+1 for correct, -0.25 for incorrect. Top 150 candidates selected.
Fees
₹1500 (Gen/OBC), ₹1200 (SC/ST), ₹0 (PwD)
Time Conducted
Typically in July
Frequency
Once a year
Result Time
Results in 4–6 weeks
