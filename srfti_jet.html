SRFTI JET

Full Form
Satyajit Ray Film and Television Institute Joint Entrance Test
Eligibility
Bachelor's degree in any stream
Scope
Admission to PG Diploma programs at SRFTI
How to Apply
Same application and exam as FTII JET (Apply at ftii.ac.in)
Exam Structure
Same structure as FTII JET (Written test (MCQs + descriptive) + interview & practical)
Score Pattern & Analysis
Same pattern – merit list based on written + interview/practical
Fees
Same as FTII JET (₹2000 Gen, ₹600 SC/ST/PwD)
Time Conducted
Same date as FTII JET (Usually March)
Frequency
Once a year
Result Time
~1 month after exam
