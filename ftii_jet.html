FTII JET

Full Form
Film and Television Institute of India Joint Entrance Test
Eligibility
Bachelor's degree in any discipline
Scope
Admission to PG Diploma programs in Film & TV (Direction, Editing, Sound, etc.) at FTII
How to Apply
Apply at ftii.ac.in
Exam Structure
Written test (MCQs + descriptive) + interview & practical
Score Pattern & Analysis
Subjective + Objective evaluation. Shortlisted for interview/practical
Fees
₹2000 (Gen), ₹600 (SC/ST/PwD)
Time Conducted
Usually March
Frequency
Once a year
Result Time
~1 month after exam
