BLOOD RELATIONS

Theory: Introduction

Blood relations means the connection between two people by blood or basically by birth. In addition to this, two people or two families can be related by marriage, hence the relations which are created by marriage are also considered as blood relations.

Blood relations can be best understood with the help of a family tree. A Family Tree is a pictorial or visual representation of our lineage. It can be considered to be like a tree where we can imagine our ancestors to be the roots, their children to be the trunk, grandchildren as branches, great grandchildren as leaves and so on.

Explanation: Solution to Example 1

Let's break down the statement: "His (the man's) brother's father" is the man's own father. "The only son of my (the woman's) grandfather" is the woman's father. So, the statement simplifies to: "The man's father is the woman's father." If they have the same father, they are siblings. The woman is the man's sister.
