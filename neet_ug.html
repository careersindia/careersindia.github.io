NEET UG

Name of Test
NEET UG
Full Form
National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (Undergraduate)
Eligibility
10+2 with PCB, English; min 50% (Gen), 40% (SC/ST/OBC), 45% (PwD); age ≥17 years
Scope
Admission to MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS, BUMS, BSMS, veterinary, and allied courses in India
How to Apply
Online via https://neet.nta.nic.in
Exam Structure
Offline (pen-paper); 720 marks; 200 questions (180 to be answered); Sections A & B in each subject
Score Pattern/Analysis
+4 for correct, -1 for wrong; percentile rank used for cut-off
Fees
₹1700 (Gen), ₹1600 (EWS/OBC), ₹1000 (SC/ST/PwD)
Time Conducted
May
Times/Year
1
Result Time
~1 month after exam
